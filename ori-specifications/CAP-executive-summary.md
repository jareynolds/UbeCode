# Executive Summary

**Description:** Balut is a product development platform that bridges ideation, storyboarding, and implementation. The platform enables teams to capture ideas, organize them visually, create structured storyboards, and maintain traceability throughout the development lifecycle.


Balut is a product development platform that bridges ideation, storyboarding, and implementation. The platform enables teams to capture ideas, organize them visually, create structured storyboards, and maintain traceability throughout the development lifecycle.

**Core Value Proposition**:
- Seamless workflow from ideation to implementation
- Complete traceability between concepts and features
- Visual, intuitive interface following Apple HIG
- Workspace-based organization
- Export capabilities for documentation
