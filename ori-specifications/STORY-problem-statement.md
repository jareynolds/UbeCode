**Challenge**: Teams often lose the connection between initial brainstorming and final implementation plans.

**Impact**:
- Ideas get lost in translation
- Context is missing from user stories
- Difficult to trace decisions back to original concepts
- Wasted time re-explaining rationale

**Solution**: Associate ideation tags with storyboard cards to maintain context and traceability throughout the product development lifecycle.
